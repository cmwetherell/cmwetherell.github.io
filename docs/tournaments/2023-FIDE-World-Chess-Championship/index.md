### Battle for the vacant throne

The 2023 FIDE World Chess Championship will be held in the city of Astana, Kazakhstan. Ian Nepomniachtchi will face off agianst Ding Liren for the vacant throne. The winner will be crowned the new FIDE World Chess Champion, replacing Magnus Carlsen who has held the title since 2013. Magnus will have held the title just shy of 10 years.
