### United States is huge favorite

Heading into the first of eleven rounds tomorrow, it's no secret that the United States is the favorite to win the Olympiad. With a rating average of 2771 they have a clear advantage over even the second ranked team, India, coming in with an average rating of 2696.

I'll update this page after every round so we can track how likely each country is to win. The predictions below are based on 1,000 simulations. You may be familiar with my coverage of the [Candidates Tournament][cand] where I was running 10,000+ simulations. Because this tournament also requires a pairing algorithm to run after every round, each smulation takes more time and I'll probably stick between 1 and 5 thousand simulations, in favor of getting the predictions out as soone as the games are over.

While the United States is the clear favorite with a 62% chance of winning, that leaves a big group of teams with reasonable winning chances. If a country is in the list below, it means they won at least once in the 1,000 simulations. If a country is not in the list below, it doesn't mean they can't win of course, just that my simulations didn't have them win any of the 1,000 runs.

The home country India will be looking to play spoiler and bring gold to a country undergoing a massive chess boom.
