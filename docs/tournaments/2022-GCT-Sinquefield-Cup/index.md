### The Chess Speaks For Itself

Hans Niemann emerges from Round 3 as the tournament leader in St Louis after a surprising win with the black pieces over world champion Magnus Carlsen. On Hikaru's stream, he was critical of Magnus' play saying "I just don't understand" what Magnus was doing in the opening.

Carlsen is still favorite to win the event, but he'll have to recover quickly in order to maintain his chances. Niemann is the points leader, but his live Elo is still well below the field making a tough road for him to win the event.

I'm back from vacation and plan to post an update after each round.
