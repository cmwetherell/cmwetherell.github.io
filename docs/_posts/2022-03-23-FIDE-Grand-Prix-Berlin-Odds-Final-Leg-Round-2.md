### Draws, draws everywhere

Today's action ended with eight draws in eight games, but there was plenty of fighting chess. 

Hikaru played with white against Esipenko and allowed a passed pawn that proved difficult to remove, but he managed to find a draw despite the engine suggesting he was a bit worse most of the game. Esipenko ran into time trouble, which may have provided Hikaru what he needed to head into round three with a draw.

After eight draws not much changes with the odds, but those with a point advantage are a little more secure in their chances to win their pool. Interestingly, there was less than a 1% chance all 8 games would end in a draw.

I've found that this early in the tournament, the odds are quite volatile so I simulated 250,000 times to try and get some credible numbers.
