### Roller-coaster day

Here's the highlights:
- Four decisive games = odds shakeup!
- Esipenko got blown off the board by Aronian, and in the interviews he said he thought he was playing Hikaru with the white pieces today.
- Aronian's win made Hikaru's qualification chances in question as it looked like he was in a drawn position.
- Hikaru grinded out a win in the endgame to create a Pool A tie between himself and Aronian.
- Round 6 is going to be great!

Other decisive results included Wesley So beating MVL with the black pieces and Sam Shankland beating Predke with the black pieces.

Rapport is still nearly a lock, and Nakamura jumped up to a 46% chance to qualify.
